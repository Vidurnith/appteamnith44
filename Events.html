HACK ON HILLS 6.0

Hackon Hills 6.0, organized by this App Team at NIT Hamirpur, was a landmark event that brought together innovators, developers, and tech enthusiasts from across the region. As North India's largest hackathon, it featured 36 hours of nonstop coding, problem-solving, and collaboration. From ideation to execution, this team led the entire initiative-creating an environment where creativity thrived and groundbreaking solutions came to life.
